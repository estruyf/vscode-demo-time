The `center` layout centers all slide content both vertically and horizontally. It is ideal for title slides, focused statements, or any slide where visual emphasis is needed.

```md
---
layout: center
---

# Welcome!

This content is centered on the slide.
```

<Aside>
The center layout applies both vertical and horizontal centering to the slide content, and sets the text alignment to center as well.
</Aside>
