The `video` layout is used to display a video as the background on the slide. It provides a large video area with content overlayed on top, perfect for creating engaging presentations with multimedia content.

## Basic Video Layout

```md
---
layout: video
video: "path/to/your/video.mp4"
---

# Your slide title

Your content here will appear over the video background.
```

## Video Properties

You can control video playback behavior using these front matter properties:

- `video` - Path to the video file (relative to workspace or absolute URL)
- `controls` - Show video controls (default: false for background videos)
- `autoplay` - Auto-play the video (default: true for background videos)
- `loop` - Loop the video (default: true for background videos)  
- `muted` - Mute the video (default: true for background videos)
- `playsInline` - Play inline on mobile devices (default: true)
- `playbackRate` - Playback speed of the video (default: 1.0)

### Background Video (Default)

By default, videos in the video layout play as background elements:

```md
---
layout: video
video: ".demo/assets/presentation-video.mp4"
---

# Welcome to Demo Time

This content appears over the video background.
```

### Interactive Video with Controls

For videos that users should interact with, enable controls:

```md
---
layout: video
video: ".demo/assets/tutorial-video.mp4"
controls: true
autoplay: false
loop: false
muted: false
---

# Video Tutorial

Watch the video below to learn more.
```

## Video Sources

### Local Video Files

```md
---
layout: video
video: ".demo/assets/my-video.mp4"
---
```

### Remote Video URLs

```md
---
layout: video
video: "https://example.com/path/to/video.mp4"
---
```

### Relative Paths

```md
---
layout: video
video: "../videos/presentation.mp4"
---
```

## Best Practices

### Performance
- Keep video files under 50MB for smooth playback
- Use compressed video formats (MP4 with H.264)
- Consider using lower resolution for background videos

### Accessibility  
- Always provide alternative content for users who cannot see videos
- Include captions for videos with audio content
- Use `muted` for background videos to prevent unexpected audio
