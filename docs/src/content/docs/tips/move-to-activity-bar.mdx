To make your demos easier to access, you can move the Demo Time view from the explorer panel to the activity bar. This way, you can have a dedicated space for your demos.

To move the Demo Time panel to the activity bar, follow these steps:

1. Open the **explorer panel**.
2. Click on the **Demo Time** view and drag it to the **activity bar**.
3. Now you have the Demo Time panel in the activity bar.
